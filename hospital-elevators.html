Hospital Elevators

Hospital elevators are used for simple transportation of a patient on wheelchair or stretcher without jerks and shocks. We manufacture hospital lift cabin, specially built for accommodating stretchers and wheel chairs. These hospital lift cabin exporter quality products can be used both in small or large hospitals. Our hospital lift cabin is fabricated to ensure proper space ad comfort for carrying stretcher. Our hospital lift cabin manufacturer products are highly resilient and have sturdy construction. These hospital lift cabin exporter products are shock and jerk-free, ensuring proper enough safety to patients and installed systems on the stretcher. We can custom manufacture hospital lift cabin as according to the specific customer requirements. We manufacture 3 types of Hospital Elevators/Lifts:

Bed cum Passenger
Features:

  • Half size mirror at the backside.
  • Acrylic False ceiling.
  • Down Collective Control (P.L.C.based).
  • Digital position indicator in car & at all floors.
  • Wooden cabin with Formica finished.
  • Stainless steel faceplates.
  • Chequered type PVC flooring.
  • M.S. Collapsible gates for car & at all floors.
  • Battery operated alarm bell & light.

Optional Features

  • Imperforated doors.
  • M.S. Powder coated cabin.

Strecher cum Passenger

  • Digital position indicator in car & at all floors.
