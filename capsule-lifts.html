Capsule Lifts

These lifts can be installed on the exterior face of the building or in the lobby. The installation of these lifts provides totally changed view to the building. The technology used in these lifts is high-tech. The range of lifts is from 8 persons to 40 persons for a three-sided glass capsule. 5 sided glass capsule is available for a minimum raise size of 13 persons. Octagonal shaped lifts are also being designed. Bottom lit marble floors, bell shaped roofs and cabin exteriors can be provided.

Our range of capsule lift cabin manufacturer product is widely used in hospitals, shopping malls, multiplexes and other residential & commercial buildings. We use qualitative capsule lift cabin manufacturer material in order to fabricate our products. Integrated with glass viewing panel, our capsule lift cabin is aesthetically designed in a capsule-like shape, hence the name. Our capsule lift cabin is exquisite in design and gives a panoramic view of outer surroundings through its glass walls. These capsule lift cabin exporter products are available in different sizes and shapes and can also be customized as according to specific requirements. Safety, security and beauty are the utmost factors kept under consideration while manufacturing this capsule lift cabin exporter product.

One exclusive feature is that glass doors can be provided. When set in a glass capsule the beauty of the capsule is further enhanced.

The lifts can be traction machine based or hydraulic machine based depending on the machine room location. Apart from the cabin all other specifications are the same as for other passenger lifts. Capsule elevators give an additional eye catching look to a modern building and act as architectural highlights of the buildings. They act like the ornaments of a building for they enhance the beauty and bring life in it. We provide capsule elevators for Hotels, Apartments, shopping malls etc.

We provide all types of capsule lifts having ultramodern design to provide a cosmic zone experience to the passengers. The design provides an extremely silent operation. It is also reliable and economical with minimum maintenance. The VVVF close loop microprocessor controller incorporated with the system makes the whole operation very sophisticated.

We are among the leading Manufacturers, Exporters and Suppliers of Capsule Lift. The Capsule Lift offered by us is available in various different models. The Capsule Lift has been widely acknowledged by the customers for its eye catch designs and unique features. These lifts are transparent which add to the attraction value. The Capsule Lift offered by us is being widely used in various shopping malls, hotels, offices and multiplexes.
